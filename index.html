The Tool

What Is
Claude Code?

Think of Claude Code as a brilliant colleague who lives inside your terminal. You describe what you want in plain English—"turn this messy spreadsheet into a client-ready dashboard"—and it does the work.

It reads your files. It writes code. It builds websites, automates reports, generates content, and creates tools tailored to your exact workflow. Built by Anthropic, the same team behind Claude AI, it's the most capable coding assistant available today.

You don't need to know how to code. You need to know how to describe what you want. That's it.

The Real Power

Skills & MCPs:
AI That Thinks Like GMR

Claude Code gets really powerful when you teach it how you work. That's what Skills are—they take expert knowledge and guide AI to act within that expertise in specific situations.

For GMR, this means taking our proprietary frameworks—how we plan activations, structure briefs, evaluate sponsorships—and turning them into Skills. Claude Code then works within those frameworks, not around them. Your thinking, amplified.

MCPs (Model Context Protocol servers) extend Claude Code even further—connecting it to your tools, databases, and internal systems so it can pull real data and take real actions.

We'll cover both. You'll see how to create them, use them, and why they're the difference between a generic AI tool and one that actually knows how GMR operates.

Guided installation with hands-on support. Troubleshooting checkpoints every 10 minutes. Success = your first "hello world."

Break · 15 min
10:30 AM
Core Skills Workshop (3 Modules)
A: Conversations & context—giving good instructions. B: File operations—reading, writing, editing. C: Building things—HTML pages, data scripts, docs. Each: 5 min explain + 15 min hands-on.
11:30 AM
Real-World GMR Use Cases

12:00

Build a personalized event landing page from scratch using Claude Code. Text description → HTML/CSS → customize → interactive elements. Checkpoints every 20 min.
2:30
Break · 15 min
2:45 PM
Choose Your Track (75 min)
Pick your path: Strategy & Analysis (competitive analysis tool), Creative & Content (multi-format content workflow), Production & Ops (event logistics automation), or Client & Account (report generator).
4:00 PM
Share-Outs
Volunteers from each track show what they built. "You built this in 75 minutes. None of you are engineers."
4:30 PM
Hackathon Challenge Reveal
Day 2's challenge drops. Teams of 2-3 form. 15 minutes to brainstorm. Judging: Practicality 30% · Creativity 25% · Execution 25% · Presentation 20%.

8:30 AM
Doors Open
